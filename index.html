SocialFurs is a private collective representing furry and therian artists, musicians, editors, and community organisers, focused on long term projects, safe collaboration, and intentional creative development.

A private creative collective built on trust, structure, and intent

SocialFurs operates as a small, curated creative organisation supporting furry and therian talent through collaboration, project coordination, and shared standards. The focus is not visibility, but sustainable creative work.

Structured by design

SocialFurs is intentionally limited in size to preserve quality, safety, and clarity of direction.

Selective membership

All members are reviewed before acceptance. This allows the collective to maintain shared expectations, age safety, and professional boundaries.

No public performance metrics

There are no follower counts, engagement systems, or algorithmic visibility. Work is evaluated on substance and reach.

What SocialFurs supports

The collective exists to support tangible creative output and responsible collaboration.

Creator representation

Members are represented through focused profiles outlining their skills, interests, and active work, without pressure to self promote constantly.

Project coordination

SocialFurs facilitates collaborative projects including editing teams, music releases, publications, and community initiatives under clear moderation.

Real world responsibility

Any offline activity connected to the collective follows explicit rules, supervision standards, and safety requirements as a baseline.

Applying to join

Access to SocialFurs is application based. This ensures alignment with the collective’s values and working standards.

Accepted applicants gain access to internal projects, coordination spaces, and ongoing creative work.
