SocialFurs is a private creative platform for furry and therian artists, musicians, editors, and community organisers, designed around safety, moderation, and responsible collaboration.

A private creative community for those who value trust and intention

SocialFurs is a curated space for furry and therian creators who want to build meaningful work, collaborate responsibly, and exist outside the noise of mainstream platforms.

Designed to stay small on purpose

Growth is not the primary goal. Stability, safety, and creative longevity are.

Curated access

Membership is reviewed manually. This allows the community to maintain clear boundaries, age safety, and shared expectations.

No public performance

There are no follower counts, no engagement races, and no algorithmic feeds. Work is shared because it matters, not because it performs.

What the platform supports

SocialFurs exists to support real creative output and real world connection.

Focused creator profiles

Profiles highlight work, interests, and ongoing projects without distraction or pressure to constantly update.

Projects and collaborations

Members organise and participate in editing teams, music projects, community initiatives, and moderated events.

Offline responsibility

When real world events exist, they are handled with clear rules, supervision, and safety as a baseline requirement.

Requesting access

Access to SocialFurs is application based. This ensures the community remains respectful, safe, and aligned.

Accepted members gain access to the full platform and ongoing projects.
